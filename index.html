What is the difference between Pressure Washing and Soft Washing?

Pressure Washing is reserved for things such as concrete or brick. Taking pressure to the side of your home can severely damage or flat out ruin the siding of your home, roof, fences, decks, etc.

We have a state of the art soft wash system that uses LOW PRESSURE not much more than a garden hose and letting our cleaning agents do the work safely for us.

What are the black streaks on my roof, can you even clean it?!

The answer is YES! The black streaks you see are more than likely what is called Gleocapsa Magma. This is an algae that will not only affect curb appeal but can also greatly diminish the integrity of your shingles. If there is biological growth on your roof, it should be remedied soon. Cleaning your roof with a pressure washer can remove the granules on the shingles and should never be done. Shingle manufacturing companies like ARMA (Asphalt Roofing Manufacturers Association) recommend a low pressure (soft wash) cleaning of your roof.
